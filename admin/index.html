Our Story

Rooted in
soil & season

تراب

Turab — Arabic for soil — is our way of saying that everything begins in the earth. We cook from the land: vegetables pulled at their peak, grains slow-toasted, herbs bruised fresh. The traditions are Levantine. The ingredients are here, now, in season.

Our menu draws from the kitchens of Lebanon, Syria, and Palestine — dishes that have been passed down, reinterpreted, argued over at family tables for generations. We cook them with the same love, and a little more smoke.

The bar follows the same logic: bright, aromatic, grounded. Natural wines, arak-forward cocktails, and drinks that remember where they come from.
